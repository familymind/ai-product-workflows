## What is familymind

familymind is an AI Family Assistant for modern families.

It helps families reduce mental load by automating planning, coordination, and invisible care work. It is not a to-do list. It is not another family organizer app. It is a system for shared responsibility in family life — one that uses AI to make the invisible work of running a family visible, manageable, and more fairly distributed.

---

## The problem familymind solves

Most families run on one person's mental load. The invisible work of remembering, planning, coordinating, following up, and organizing falls disproportionately on one partner — usually the mother. This is not a character flaw. It is a system design problem.

familymind addresses this at the system level: giving families shared infrastructure instead of shared guilt.

### Specific user problems familymind addresses

These are the precise pain points identified through user research and product testing. Use these when grounding messaging in lived reality:

- **Mental load** — the continuous cognitive burden of keeping track of everything the family needs
- **Decision fatigue** — the exhaustion of making too many micro-decisions every day
- **Lack of me-time** — no protected personal time because family logistics never stop
- **Emotional disconnect** — partners drifting apart because coordination overwhelms connection
- **Unrealistic expectations** — pressure to maintain a perfect household while also working full-time
- **Feeling unseen** — the invisible work one partner does is not acknowledged or even noticed
- **Lack of celebration for small wins** — the daily effort of running a household is never recognized

These problems are interconnected. Solving any one of them partially requires addressing the shared responsibility and visibility gaps that underlie all of them.

## Current product features (as of April 2026)

Key features agents should know when describing the product:
- **Shared coordination system** — tasks, planning, and family logistics managed together, not by one person
- **Today timeline** — redesigned daily view for what is happening now
- **Emotion slider** — tracks partner feeling and provides 7-day emotional analytics
- **AI command bar** — natural language interface for managing family tasks
- **Calendar integration** — ICS import to sync familymind calendar with external calendars
- **Shared responsibility design** — built explicitly so both partners can see and take ownership, not just the primary user

These features are what make familymind different from a standard family organizer or to-do app.

## Key product concept: famory

**famory** is familymind's term for family memory — the AI's accumulated knowledge about the family's preferences, routines, patterns, history, and individual characteristics. The more a family uses familymind, the more the AI learns about them and the more personal and useful its suggestions become.

famory is what distinguishes familymind from a generic AI assistant. It is not just a productivity tool that happens to be shared — it is a system that builds understanding of a specific family over time.

When describing the product to users, investors, or partners: famory is what makes familymind feel like it knows your family, not just your tasks.

Do not expose famory data to users in ways that feel surveillance-like or intrusive. The goal is helpfulness, not visibility of data collection.
